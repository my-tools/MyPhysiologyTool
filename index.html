Description

Incremental game to aid learning of human physiology

  • Begin system with clicks ("Click to beat heart")
  • Generate resources to setup automation ("Install cardiovascular system in medulla oblongata to automate heart beat")
  • Tweak automation when problems arise ("Create relationship between oxygen levels and heart rate")
  • Tab to new organ systems
  • Healthy options (meals and exercise) require some degree of inconvenience (100 clicks versus 1 click to eat fast food)
  • Unhealthy lifestyles create problems within systems build up until they are encountered
